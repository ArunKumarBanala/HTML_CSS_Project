About Malayamarutham

       Malayamarutham Upcoming Singers Academy (MUSA) is a non-profit organisation which provides training in Light Music (Film songs, Geet, Ghazals, group songs, patriotic songs, folk songs and traditional songs that depict our Indian culture, light classical music, Annamayya, Purandaradas, Ramadas Keerthanas etc). Imparting techniques of singing like breath management, modulation, emoting while keeping shruti and laya in mind, performance techniques like body language, holding mic, voice throwing etc through real time exposure to stage and voice recording studios.

Our Founder

Ramaiyer Viswanath, a talented playback singer
and music director founded Malayamarutham
Upcoming Singers Academy in Hyderabad,
Telangana, India. He is known for melodious
rendition and compositions of devotional
and light music for movies, television and
radio shows
