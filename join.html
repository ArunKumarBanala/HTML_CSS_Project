The Temple of music for all those children with passion to sing and meeting below criteria

  1. The child should be between First class to Intermediate ( less than 18 years)
  2. Child Should have strong knowledge on Saraliswarams, Jantaswarams, Hechu Sthayee Swarams, Thachu Sthayee Swarams, Dhatu Swarams, Geetams in carnatic music
  3. There would be an audition for every child who wants to join Malayamarutham
  4. He/she needs to record a melody song (only pallavi and charanam) and first Sarali Swaram and upload on the website (please refer to the right side of the page for link to upload)
  5. Fill the form on right side of the page
  6. Our guru Sri Viswanath Sir would review the song uploaded
  7. If selected the child can join Malayamarutham classes every Thursday and Sunday at specified location time. Information about the selection, location & time will be sent through an Email
