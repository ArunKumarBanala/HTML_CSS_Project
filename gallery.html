         Malayamarutham has been growing by leaps and bounds. In the able guidance of Shri R. Viswanath garu, Malayamarutham alumni has reached great heights and its not an exaggeration if we say, most of them are ruling the Telugu film industry today. They joined Malayamarutham as toddlers and today it gives us immense pleasure to see them fulfil their dreams. Children of Malayamarutham also participated in and won titles in reality shows and competitions bring accolades to both their parents and their mentor & guide Shri Viswanath garu.

Ramaiyer Viswanath, a talented playback singer and music director founded Malayamarutham Upcoming Singers Academy in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. He is known for melodiousrendition and compositions of devotional and light music for movies, television and radio shows.
