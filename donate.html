Malayamarutham Building Fund

           One of our core activity has been in field of music, where we intend to find right talent and train children to help them achieve their goals. From a humble background of around 10 students back in 2007, today we are proud to have close to 100 students in various stages of their musical journey. Our constant endeavor is to provide these children a serene environment where they can impart their training. As part of that initiative, we would like to have a place of our own to continue doing this service. To make it simple for people who wish to donate, we launched a campaign called ‘Donate a Yard’, where the patrons can donate an approximate fixed cost towards a Yard of land.

Our vision is to create a social impact, which would not be possible without your help Make a donation to any of our activities, or join our Malayamarutham Building Fund campaign.
